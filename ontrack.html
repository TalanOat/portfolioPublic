Project Goals

Primary Goal

The main objective of the project was to equip users with tools to track their fitness goals and nutritional information, enabling them to make more informed decisions.

Secondary Goal

The other objective was to provide web-based platform to reach the widest audience, ensuring accessibility from any device, regardless of its capabilities or updates. This feature sets the project apart from many alternatives, allowing users to input information seamlessly from both mobile devices and desktop computers, or any device with browser support.

Summary

Initially a team collaboration, this project evolved into a solo effort to create a fitness and diet tracking website. The platform helps users track fitness goals, analyse nutritional data, and visualise progress with achievements and milestones. It aims to deliver evidence based data and targets to allow users to become healthier and stay on track to their fitness goals.

Starting as a backend developer, I later took over the project to modernise its design and enhance user experience with my UX design skills. I rebuilt the dashboard from scratch using React.

What I Took Away

Teamwork

Working with a team was a crucial aspect of this project, as it required us to work collaboratively to complete objectives and deliver a high-quality product. This experience reinforced my ability to communicate effectively with others and collaborate effectively in a team setting.

Mobile and Desktop

I further developed my skills in translating complex designs into user-friendly, accessible websites optimised for both mobile and desktop. My approach focused on preserving familiar functionality to ensure ease of use for the end user.

RESTful API Design

To enable dashboard functionality, I developed a RESTful API to bridge the frontend and backend. I implemented a Firebase-powered database for scalable, cost-effective storage of user data and information.

What Could be added

Intergration with Other Apps

The project could be enhanced by allowing a connection with other fitness tracking apps, such as the Apple Health app, to provide more information and build a more comprehensive user experience.

Social Connectivity

Introducing leaderboards and social features could significantly enhance user engagement and motivation. Similar to the app: Strava, these features could foster a sense of community by allowing users to connect, compete, and join local groups.
